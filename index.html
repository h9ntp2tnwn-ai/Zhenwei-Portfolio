Who I Am

About me

I'm Zhenwei Li, a student at Oakland Technical High School with a 4.0 GPA and Student of the Month recognition. I show up fully — whether that's on the badminton court, in community service, or as a troop leader.

I intern at YMHA conducting psychology research and completed Adolescent Crisis Response Training. I also work at Kumon as a teaching assistant, supporting fellow students in Math and English. As a Boy Scout Senior Patrol Leader, I've been leading my troop since 2022.

I'm fluent in Chinese (Mandarin & Cantonese) and passionate about STEM, Health, and Business. This page is a snapshot of what I've built — there's more to come.
